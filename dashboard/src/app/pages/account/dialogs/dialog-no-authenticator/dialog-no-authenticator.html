What to Do if You Don’t Have an Authenticator App

  1. Download the Authenticator App:
    • For iPhone: Go to the App Store and search for “Google Authenticator” or “Authy.”
    • For Android: Go to the Google Play Store and search for “Google Authenticator” or “Authy.”
  2. Install the App: Click on “Download” or “Install” to add it to your phone.
  3. Set Up the App: Open the app and follow the on-screen instructions to set it up. You will be asked to link it to your account by scanning the QR code on your computer screen.
